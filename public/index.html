ABOUT GIVE HELP

This is a non-profit organization that aim in helping less priledge people.

Child Care

In most cases children are taken care of by their parents, legal guardians, or siblings. In some cases, it is also seen that children care for other children. For children without parent (orphans), we do our part by providing help for them.

Educate Children

In Nigeria, about 10.5 million children are not in school even though primary education is officially free and compulsory. We do our part by providing help to ensure educational provision is available for orphans.

Physical Growth

From birth to about age 1 or 2 years, children grow rapidly. After this time, growth slows. As growth slows, children need fewer calories and parents may notice a decrease in appetite. We also try to provide food for orphans.
