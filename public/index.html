About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

This non-profit organization was created by Olajide Joy Inioluwa. It started as a whatsapp group where group of people with same mindset of helping the orphans by donating to the orphanage at Ilorin. We also wish to touch life nationwide as God help us growing.

However, group of few people can't do it alone, so we decided to ask people for help in order to help the orphanages more. We don't also want to bring worry on people who wish to help about the amount so we ask for just ₦200 only, and if you are enable to help more, you can do so. May God help us all.

This website was developed by Programmer G-WIN for Valentine with Orphans. It enable people to surf for our live infomation, make payment for the orphanage donation, and also contact us.
